Call for Mentors: Intellect Africa Scholars Program

Objectives
  • Build a community of young Africans who are passionate about learning.
  • Share knowledge, skills, and experiences with other young Africans.
  • Match mentor with mentees based on their majors and career interests.
  • Require mentors to meet with their mentees at least once in two weeks for the first two months.
  • Allow meetings to be scheduled as needed throughout the duration of the program with mentees.
